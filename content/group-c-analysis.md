# Group C Analysis — World Cup 2026

## 🏟️ Group C: Brazil 🇧🇷 | Morocco 🇲🇦 | Scotland 🏴 | Haiti 🇭🇹

### Overview
THE group of the tournament so far. Brazil vs Morocco is a rematch of one of the greatest World Cup stories — Morocco's 2022 semi-final run. Scotland return to the big stage, and Haiti make a fairy-tale appearance.

---

### 🇧🇷 Brazil
**World Cup Pedigree:** Record 5 titles. THE World Cup team.
**Key Strength:** Endless attacking talent. Vinicius Jr, Rodrygo, Endrick.
**Key Weakness:** Defensive questions, haven't won since 2002
**Verdict:** Should top the group, but Morocco will make them sweat.

### 🇲🇦 Morocco
**World Cup Pedigree:** 2022 semi-finalists — the greatest African World Cup run ever
**Key Strength:** Defensive organization is elite. Hakimi, Amrabat, Ziyech generation.
**Key Weakness:** Can they replicate 2022 magic? Or was it a one-off?
**Verdict:** Genuine contenders to beat Brazil. This is must-watch football.

### 🏴 Scotland
**World Cup Pedigree:** First World Cup since 1998
**Key Strength:** Fighting spirit, Premier League-based squad, tactical discipline
**Key Weakness:** Lack of individual star power at the highest level
**Verdict:** Will compete hard but likely 3rd. Could nick a result if others underperform.

### 🇭🇹 Haiti
**World Cup Pedigree:** First appearance since 1974 — 52-year wait!
**Key Strength:** The story. Raw passion. Nothing to lose.
**Key Weakness:** Biggest quality gap in the group
**Verdict:** Here for the experience. Every goal they score will be historic.
