## The Story
Uzbekistan are Central Asia's football powerhouse — and after decades of agonizing near-misses, they've finally qualified for a World Cup. The White Wolves have been Asian football's greatest "nearly" team: consistently competitive in AFC qualifying but always falling just short. Until now.

The timing is poetic. Uzbekistan's youth development has produced a golden generation of technically gifted players, several now in European leagues. Group K is tough — Portugal and Colombia are genuine contenders — but Uzbekistan's debut energy and tactical quality could produce surprises.
