# South Africa 🇿🇦 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~60
- **Confederation:** CAF
- **World Cup Appearances:** 4
- **Best Result:** Group stage (2010 — hosts; beat France)
- **Group:** A (vs Mexico, South Korea, UEFA Playoff D)

## The Story
Bafana Bafana return to the World Cup for the first time since hosting in 2010 — a tournament remembered for vuvuzelas, Siphiwe Tshabalala's stunning opening goal, and beating France in the group stage. South African football has experienced ups and downs since, but qualifying for 2026 marks a genuine revival.

Group A alongside co-hosts Mexico and South Korea is tough but not impossible. South Africa's physicality, pace, and unpredictability make them a wildcard.

## Playing Style
- Physical, fast, unpredictable — African flair with Southern African grit
- Direct attacking play through pace on the flanks
- Improving defensive organization
- Passionate crowd support — South Africans in North America will be loud
- Capable of individual brilliance in big moments

## Key Players to Watch
- A mix of domestic PSL stars and emerging European-based talent
- Physical forwards with pace
- Improving goalkeeping and defensive stability

## Strengths
✅ 2010 host experience — know the World Cup stage
✅ Beat France at 2010 World Cup — capable of upsets
✅ Physical and fast — match anyone athletically
✅ Passionate support from diaspora and neutrals
✅ Nothing to lose — classic underdog energy

## Weaknesses

❌ Defensive fragility against technical teams
