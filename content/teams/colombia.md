# Colombia 🇨🇴 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** 13
- **Confederation:** CONMEBOL
- **World Cup Appearances:** 7
- **Best Result:** Quarter-finals (2014)
- **Group:** K (vs Portugal, IC Playoff 1 winner, Uzbekistan)
- **Key Question:** Can Copa América 2024 finalists translate that form to the World Cup?

## The Story
Colombia are riding a wave. 2024 Copa América finalists, 3rd place in CONMEBOL World Cup qualifying (behind Argentina and Ecuador), and a squad bursting with Premier League and European talent. Luis Díaz has become one of the world's most exciting wingers at Liverpool, and the supporting cast runs deep.

Group K is brutal — Portugal are ranked 6th — but Colombia have the quality to compete with anyone. The 2014 World Cup, where James Rodríguez won the Golden Boot and Colombia reached the quarter-finals, proved this team belongs on the biggest stage. This generation wants to go further.

## Playing Style
- South American flair with European tactical discipline
- Explosive on the counter — Díaz's pace terrifies full-backs
- Physical and aggressive midfield pressing
- Set-piece quality — dangerous from dead balls
- Emotional, passionate, ride the momentum of the crowd

## Key Players to Watch
- **Luis Díaz** — Liverpool's Colombian rocket. Pace, skill, end product. The talisman.
- **Jhon Durán** — Young striker making waves in the Premier League. Power and instinct.
- **Richard Ríos** — Dynamic midfielder, engine of the team
- **Jhon Arias** — Versatile attacker, consistent performer
- **Dávinson Sánchez** — Experienced defender, aerial dominance

## Strengths
✅ 2024 Copa América finalists — peak form entering the tournament
✅ Luis Díaz is a genuine world-class difference maker
✅ CONMEBOL 3rd place — battle-tested against the best
✅ Deep squad with European experience across multiple leagues
✅ Colombian fans create incredible atmosphere — diaspora in the US is huge

## Weaknesses
❌ Portugal in the group makes qualification a battle, not a formality
❌ Can be emotionally volatile — red cards, discipline issues
❌ Inconsistent away from South America historically
❌ No World Cup semi-final in history — the ceiling hasn't been broken
❌ Goalkeeper position has been a recurring question mark

## World Cup History Highlights
- 🏆 2014: Quarter-finals — James Rodríguez Golden Boot (6 goals), lost to Brazil
- 📊 2018: Round of 16 — lost to England on penalties
- 😤 2022: Failed to qualify — shocking collapse in CONMEBOL qualifying
- ⚡ 2024 Copa América: Finalists — lost to Argentina in extra time
