# Inter-Confederation Playoff Path 2 — TBD Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **Possible Teams:** Iraq 🇮🇶, Bolivia 🇧🇴, Suriname 🇸🇷
- **Playoff Dates:** Semi-finals March 26, Final March 31, 2026
- **Group:** I (vs France, Senegal, Norway)

## Scenario Analysis

### If Iraq 🇮🇶 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~55 | First WC since 1986 — 40-year gap!
- **Impact:** Adds passionate Middle Eastern flavor to Group I. Competitive but France/Senegal/Norway are stronger.
- **Key Players:** Growing pool of Gulf league and European talent
- **Story:** Iraq returning to the World Cup after 40 years — in a nation that has endured war and rebuilding. Enormously emotional.
- **Betting shift:** Iraq vs Norway for 3rd could be interesting. Mostly unchanged for top 2.

### If Bolivia 🇧🇴 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~80 | First WC since 1994
- **Impact:** Bolivia's altitude warriors at sea level — historically struggle away from La Paz (3,600m).
- **Key Players:** Domestic league-based squad
- **Story:** 1 World Cup win in their entire history (vs South Korea, 1994). Returning after 32 years.
- **Betting shift:** Weakest option for Group I. France, Senegal, Norway advance.

### If Suriname 🇸🇷 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~120 | Population ~600,000 — historic debut
- **Impact:** Smallest nation in the group by far. Limited quality.
- **Key Players:** Dutch-born players with Surinamese heritage (similar to Curaçao pipeline)
- **Story:** The land that produced Gullit, Rijkaard, Seedorf, Davids — Surinamese heritage but now with their own team at a World Cup. Incredible narrative.
- **Betting shift:** Group I effectively becomes a 3-team race.

## 💰 Betting Guidance
Iraq qualifying is the only scenario that slightly shifts Group I dynamics. Bolivia and Suriname are unlikely to challenge the top 3.
