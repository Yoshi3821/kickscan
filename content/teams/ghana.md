# Ghana 🇬🇭 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** 72
- **Confederation:** CAF
- **World Cup Appearances:** 5
- **Best Result:** Quarter-finals (2010)
- **Group:** L (vs England, Croatia, Panama)

## The Story
Ghana's 2010 World Cup quarter-final remains one of football's most dramatic moments — Suárez's handball on the line, Gyan's missed penalty, an entire continent's dreams crushed. The Black Stars have never recaptured that magic. Qatar 2022 was poor (one win, bottom of group), and the team is in a rebuilding phase.

Group L is unforgiving: England and Croatia are both genuine contenders. Ghana will be battling Panama for 3rd place, with an outside chance of an upset result against the European heavyweights.

## Playing Style
- Athletic, fast, technically gifted attackers
- Exciting but inconsistent — capable of brilliance and collapse in the same game
- Physical presence and aerial threat
- Counter-attacking speed through pacey wingers
- Emotional — rise to the occasion or fall flat

## Key Players to Watch
- Emerging Premier League and European talent
- Athletic forwards with pace and power
- Young defenders developing in European leagues

## Strengths
✅ 2010 quarter-final pedigree — proved they can compete at the highest level
✅ Athletic and fast — can trouble any defense on the counter
✅ Beat South Korea 3-2 at 2022 World Cup — capable of excitement
✅ Passionate diaspora in North America — incredible fan support

## Weaknesses
❌ Recent form has dipped — poor 2022 (bottom of group overall)
❌ England and Croatia are significantly stronger
❌ Defensive fragility — concede too many goals
❌ Generational transition — 2010 heroes long gone
❌ Consistency is the fundamental issue
