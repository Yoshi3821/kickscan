# Switzerland 🇨🇭 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~20
- **Confederation:** UEFA
- **World Cup Appearances:** 13
- **Best Result:** Quarter-finals (1934, 1938, 1954, 2024 Euros)
- **Group:** B (vs Canada, Qatar, UEFA Playoff A winner)
- **Key Question:** Can the Swiss maintain their status as Europe's most consistent dark horse?

## The Story
Switzerland are the definition of solid. Not flashy, not headline-grabbing, but consistently competitive. They've qualified for every major tournament since 2014 and always seem to advance from the group stage. Euro 2024 quarter-finalists (beat Italy in the Round of 16), and perennial overperformers relative to expectations.

Group B alongside co-hosts Canada, Qatar, and a UEFA playoff winner is manageable. Switzerland should be favorites for 1st place — a rare position for a team that thrives as underdogs.

## Playing Style
- Organized, disciplined, tactically astute
- Strong defensive foundation — rarely concede sloppy goals
- Quick transitions and clinical finishing
- Xhaka as the midfield metronome — controls tempo and dictates play
- Set-piece quality — well-drilled from dead balls

## Key Players to Watch
- **Granit Xhaka** — Bayer Leverkusen's midfield leader. Transformed under Alonso.
- **Breel Embolo** — Physical striker, pace and power
- **Dan Ndoye** — Emerging winger with speed and directness
- **Manuel Akanji** — Man City's reliable center-back
- **Yann Sommer** — One of Europe's best goalkeepers. Shot-stopping excellence.

## Strengths
✅ Consistent tournament performers — always qualify, always competitive
✅ Xhaka is world-class after Leverkusen transformation
✅ Akanji brings Man City-level composure to the backline
✅ Sommer in goal is a massive safety net
✅ Group B is favorable — could top it

## Weaknesses
❌ Lack of a world-class striker — goals come by committee
❌ Can struggle against truly elite opposition in knockouts
❌ "Boring" reputation means they're overlooked — good for betting, bad for hype
❌ Aging core — Xhaka, Shaqiri (if selected) approaching twilight
❌ Being favorites in the group is unfamiliar territory

## 💰 Betting Outlook
- **To win the group:** Fair favorite ~1.80 — should edge Canada
- **To qualify (top 2):** Near-certain ~1.25 — Group B is the easiest in the tournament
- **To reach Quarter-finals:** Value at ~3.50 — depends on Round of 32 draw
- **Switzerland vs Canada:** THE group decider. Draw or Switzerland win — tight and tactical.
