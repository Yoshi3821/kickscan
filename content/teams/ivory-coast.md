# Ivory Coast 🇨🇮 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** 42
- **Confederation:** CAF
- **World Cup Appearances:** 4
- **Best Result:** Group stage (0 knockouts in 3 attempts)
- **Group:** E (vs Germany, Curaçao, Ecuador)

## The Story
The Elephants return with one clear mission: get out of the group stage for the first time. Ivory Coast have never advanced past the group at a World Cup — a frustrating record for a nation that has produced Drogba, Yaya Touré, and Gervinho. As reigning AFCON 2023 champions (won on home soil), they arrive with genuine belief.

Group E features Germany (vulnerable after two group exits) and Ecuador (CONMEBOL runners-up). It's tough but crackable — Ivory Coast have the pace, power, and tactical quality to compete.

## Playing Style
- Athletic, fast, physically dominant — impossible to outmuscle
- Counter-attacking with devastating pace on the wings
- AFCON-winning tactical discipline and team cohesion
- Set-piece threat — aerial dominance from corners
- Emotional, passionate — ride momentum in big moments

## Key Players to Watch
- Premier League and Ligue 1 talent across the squad
- Young, dynamic attackers with pace and directness
- Experienced defensive spine from top European leagues

## Strengths
✅ AFCON 2023 champions — peak winning mentality
✅ Athletic and physically imposing — match anyone for speed and power
✅ Germany are vulnerable — two straight group exits make them beatable
✅ Counter-attacking quality can hurt any defense
✅ Nothing to lose — underdog energy with real quality

## Weaknesses
❌ 0 knockout appearances in 3 World Cups — the African curse continues
❌ Ecuador's CONMEBOL quality is a different challenge from African opponents
❌ Defensive inconsistency against elite technical teams
❌ Squad depth beyond starting XI drops off
❌ Can be emotionally volatile — discipline issues

## 💰 Betting Outlook
- **To qualify (top 2):** Tough ~3.50 — Germany and Ecuador are favored
- **Ivory Coast vs Ecuador:** THE group decider for 2nd. Draw is the smart play.
- **Ivory Coast to beat Germany:** Longshot ~6.00 — but Japan and South Korea did it recently.
- **Ivory Coast vs Curaçao — Over 2.5:** Should dominate and score freely.
