# Iran 🇮🇷 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~22
- **Confederation:** AFC
- **World Cup Appearances:** 7
- **Best Result:** Group stage (best: 1 win, 1998/2018/2022)
- **Group:** G (vs Belgium, Egypt, New Zealand)
- **Key Question:** Can Iran finally break through to the knockout stage?

## The Story
Iran are Asia's most consistent World Cup qualifiers — 7 appearances and counting. Yet they've never made it past the group stage. Every tournament brings hope, and every tournament ends the same way. At Qatar 2022, they beat Wales 2-0 and were minutes away from eliminating the USA, only to lose 1-0 in the final group game.

Group G offers perhaps their best chance. Belgium are beatable (2022 showed golden generations can implode), Egypt are competitive but not dominant, and New Zealand are the weakest team in the group. If Iran are ever going to break through, this could be the tournament.

## Playing Style
- Ultra-organized defensive structure — one of the hardest teams to score against
- Counter-attacking with clinical efficiency — don't need much possession to win
- Physical, aggressive, win every aerial duel
- Set-piece mastery — one of the best in the tournament from corners and free kicks
- Discipline and patience — happy to play ugly, grind 1-0 wins

## Key Players to Watch
- **Mehdi Taremi** — Inter Milan/top European club striker. Clinical finisher, physical presence.
- **Sardar Azmoun** — "The Iranian Messi." Pace, skill, goals.
- **Alireza Jahanbakhsh** — Experienced winger, big-game temperament
- **Ehsan Hajsafi** — Veteran captain, defensive steel

## Strengths
✅ 7th World Cup appearance — massive experience
✅ Defensive organization is among the best in Asia
✅ Beat Wales at 2022 World Cup — big-game credentials
✅ Physical dominance in aerial battles
✅ Group G is manageable — realistic chance to qualify

## Weaknesses
❌ Never made it past the group stage — the psychological barrier
❌ Limited creativity — struggle to create chances against deep defenses
❌ Over-reliance on defensive structure means any goal conceded is often fatal
❌ Off-field political dynamics can affect team focus
❌ Aging core — generational transition ongoing

## 💰 Betting Outlook
- **To qualify (top 2):** Value at ~3.00 — Belgium and Egypt are beatable
- **Iran vs Egypt — Under 2.5 goals:** Defensive masterclass. 1-0 or 0-0 guaranteed vibes.
- **Iran vs New Zealand — Iran win:** Should be comfortable. Bankable result.
- **Iran to finish above Egypt:** ~3.50 — defensive discipline could edge it.

## KickScan Verdict
Iran are the ultimate "hard to beat" team. They won't thrill anyone with attacking football, but they'll grind, frustrate, and steal results. Group G gives them a genuine path to the knockout stage for the first time. If they beat New Zealand and draw with Egypt, they could qualify with 5-7 points. **Prediction: 3rd in group, but capable of 2nd if everything clicks.**
