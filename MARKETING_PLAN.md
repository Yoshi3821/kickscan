## 10. CRITICAL RULES

1. **NEVER spam.** Provide value first. Links second. Getting banned from Reddit or forums kills a channel permanently.
2. **Be consistent.** 80% of marketing success is showing up daily. Don't post for 3 days then disappear for a week.
3. **Ride the news cycle.** When World Cup news breaks (draw, qualifying, injury), be the FIRST to post about it from a betting angle.
4. **Data > Opinions.** Your competitive advantage is AI + data. Lead with numbers, not hot takes.
5. **Build a personal brand alongside the site.** People follow people, not logos. Have a personality on Twitter/TikTok.
6. **Cross-pollinate.** Every blog post becomes a Twitter thread becomes a TikTok becomes a Reddit analysis becomes a newsletter item.
7. **Track everything.** Use UTM parameters on all shared links: `kickscan.io?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch`
