### Overview
Co-hosts Canada get a very workable group. Switzerland are the experienced campaigners, Qatar return after hosting in 2022, and the UEFA playoff winner (likely Italy) could make this group explosive.

---

### 🇨🇦 Canada — The Co-Hosts
**World Cup Pedigree:** 2nd appearance (first since 1986, returned in 2022)
**Key Strength:** Alphonso Davies is world-class. Jonathan David scores goals. Home support.
**Key Weakness:** Defensive depth, tournament inexperience
**Verdict:** Should qualify. Home advantage + growing program = 2nd place minimum.

### 🇨🇭 Switzerland
**World Cup Pedigree:** Consistent qualifiers, Round of 16 regulars
**Key Strength:** Organized, disciplined, never easy to beat. Xhaka leads the midfield.
**Key Weakness:** Rarely beat top teams in knockouts
**Verdict:** Favorites to top the group on quality alone.

### 🇶🇦 Qatar
**World Cup Pedigree:** 2022 hosts — went out in group stage without a win
**Key Strength:** Asian Cup winners, technical players
**Key Weakness:** Struggled badly at 2022 World Cup despite home advantage
**Verdict:** Likely 3rd or 4th. Need massive improvement from 2022.

### UEFA Playoff A Winner (Italy / Wales / Bosnia / N. Ireland)

**If Italy:** This group becomes STACKED. Italy bring tournament DNA, tactical mastery.
**If Wales:** Bale retired, but still competitive
**Verdict:** If Italy qualify, they're favorites to top the group.
