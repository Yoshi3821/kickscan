# Norway 🇳🇴 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** 29
- **Confederation:** UEFA
- **World Cup Appearances:** 4 (first since 1998 — 28-year gap!)
- **Best Result:** Round of 16 (1998)
- **Group:** I (vs France, Senegal, IC Playoff 2 winner)
- **Key Question:** Can Erling Haaland deliver on the World Cup stage?

## The Story
Norway haven't been at a World Cup since 1998 — the year they beat Brazil 2-1 in the group stage. Twenty-eight years later, they return with the biggest reason for excitement in their football history: Erling Haaland. The Manchester City striker is arguably the most devastating goalscorer on the planet, and the World Cup is the only major stage he hasn't conquered.

The squad beyond Haaland isn't bad either. Martin Ødegaard (Arsenal's captain) provides elite creativity, and a generation of Scandinavian talent has emerged across Europe's top leagues. But the gap between Haaland/Ødegaard and the rest of the squad is the concern. In Group I, France are untouchable favorites and Senegal are formidable — Norway are fighting for 2nd place.

## Playing Style
- Everything through Haaland — get him the ball, he scores
- Ødegaard's creativity and set-piece delivery feeds the machine
- Counter-attacking focus — sit deep, spring Haaland on the break
- Physical Scandinavian style — tall, strong, aerial threat
- Disciplined defensive shape when without the ball

## Key Players to Watch
- **Erling Haaland** — Man City's goal machine. 50+ goals a season. The World Cup's most anticipated individual storyline.

## Strengths
✅ Erling Haaland — the best striker in the world, full stop
✅ Ødegaard is Arsenal's captain — world-class creativity
✅ First World Cup in 28 years — squad will be massively motivated
✅ No pressure of expectations — classic underdog energy
✅ Physical and aerial dominance — Scandinavian athletes

## Weaknesses
❌ Squad depth beyond Haaland + Ødegaard drops off significantly
❌ France AND Senegal in the group — brutal draw
❌ No World Cup experience for 28 years — tournament management is unknown
❌ Defensive quality is the weak link — can they contain Mbappé?
❌ If Haaland is marked out of games, Plan B is limited
