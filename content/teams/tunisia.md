# Tunisia 🇹🇳 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~38
- **Confederation:** CAF
- **World Cup Appearances:** 7
- **Best Result:** Group stage (0 knockouts in 6 attempts)
- **Group:** F (vs Netherlands, Japan, UEFA Playoff B winner)

## The Story
Tunisia are Africa's most frequent World Cup visitors alongside Cameroon — 7 appearances and counting. The problem? They've never made it past the group stage. Not once. It's become Tunisia's defining World Cup narrative: consistently qualify, consistently exit in the groups.

That said, they beat France at Qatar 2022 (though already eliminated) and drew with Denmark. They're competitive against anyone for 90 minutes — the issue is sustaining it across three group games. Group F is brutal: Netherlands and Japan are elite, and even the UEFA playoff team could be Poland or Ukraine.

## Playing Style
- Defensively organized — North African tactical discipline
- Physical and aggressive pressing
- Counter-attacking quality through pace on the wings
- Set-piece specialists — dangerous from free kicks and corners
- Emotional intensity — feed off the crowd and big moments

## Key Players to Watch
- European-based talent from Ligue 1, Bundesliga, and Serie A
- Strong goalkeeping tradition
- Physical midfield presence

## Strengths
