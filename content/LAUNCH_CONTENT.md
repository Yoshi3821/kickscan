### Question: "How to bet on the FIFA World Cup 2026?"

**Answer:**
```
Here's a beginner-friendly guide to World Cup 2026 betting:

**1. Understand the bet types:**
- Match result (1X2): Home win, draw, or away win
- Over/under goals: Total goals in a match
- Both teams to score (BTTS)
- Correct score
- Outright winner: Who wins the whole tournament
- Top scorer: Golden Boot winner

**2. Always compare odds:**
Different sportsbooks offer different odds for the same match. The difference can be 10-20% on your returns. Use an odds comparison tool to find the best price.

**3. Start with group stage value:**
Groups are the easiest to analyze because you have clear rankings, head-to-head data, and qualifying form. Look for mispriced 2nd-place finishers.

**4. Watch for arbitrage:**
When bookmakers disagree strongly on odds, sometimes you can bet all outcomes and guarantee profit regardless of result. This is called arbitrage betting and it's perfectly legal.

**5. Bankroll management:**
Never bet more than 2-5% of your total bankroll on a single match. The World Cup has 104 matches — pace yourself.

There are free tools that compare odds across bookmakers and even find arbitrage opportunities automatically. I recommend using one before placing any bets.
```
