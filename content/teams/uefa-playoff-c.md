## Scenario Analysis

- **FIFA Ranking:** ~28 | Passionate, talented, unpredictable
- **Impact:** Group D gets interesting. Turkey could genuinely challenge for 2nd behind the USA.
- **Key Players:** Güler (Real Madrid), Çalhanoğlu (Inter), Yıldız (Juventus)
- **Betting shift:** 2nd place becomes a four-way battle. Turkey's flair vs Paraguay's grit vs Australia's physicality.
- **Story:** Young Turkish talent (Güler, Yıldız) arriving on the world stage.

### If Romania 🇷🇴 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~35 | Euro 2024 surprise — topped their group
- **Impact:** Disciplined, organized, dangerous on the counter. Competitive for 2nd.
- **Key Players:** Drăgușin (Spurs), Man, Stanciu
- **Betting shift:** Romania could push Paraguay and Australia for 2nd.

### If Slovakia 🇸🇰 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~45 | Solid, experienced, Euro 2024 Round of 16
- **Impact:** Competitive but limited. 3rd/4th likely.
- **Key Players:** Škriniar, Lobotka, Haraslín

### If Kosovo 🇽🇰 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~100 | Historic first World Cup
- **Impact:** Weakest outcome. USA, Paraguay, Australia advance.
- **Story:** A nation that only joined FIFA in 2016 — incredible if they qualify.
