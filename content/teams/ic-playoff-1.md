## Scenario Analysis

### If DR Congo 🇨🇩 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~60 | Massive African talent, first WC as DR Congo (Zaire in 1974)
- **Impact:** Adds genuine quality and physicality to Group K. Could push Uzbekistan for 3rd.
- **Key Players:** Premier League and Ligue 1 talent through the Congolese diaspora
- **Story:** A nation of 100M people with incredible football passion. Huge diaspora support in North America.
- **Betting shift:** 3rd place becomes DR Congo vs Uzbekistan — competitive.

### If Jamaica 🇯🇲 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~55 | The Reggae Boyz return (last WC: 1998)
- **Impact:** CONCACAF physicality and Premier League dual-nationality pipeline.
- **Key Players:** Players with English football connections through dual nationality
- **Story:** Caribbean vibes at a World Cup — incredible atmosphere guaranteed.
- **Betting shift:** Jamaica vs Uzbekistan for 3rd — close.

### If New Caledonia 🇳🇨 Qualifies
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~160 | French overseas territory, ~270,000 people
- **Impact:** By far the weakest option. Portugal and Colombia unaffected.
- **Story:** The ultimate fairy tale — the smallest territory ever at a World Cup. French connections.
- **Betting shift:** Group K becomes effectively a 3-team group.

## 💰 Betting Guidance
DR Congo or Jamaica qualifying makes Group K's 3rd-place race interesting. New Caledonia qualifying has minimal impact on the top 2.
