# Mexico 🇲🇽 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~15
- **Confederation:** CONCACAF
- **World Cup Appearances:** 17 (co-hosts 2026)
- **Best Result:** Quarter-finals (1970, 1986 — both as hosts)
- **Group:** A (vs South Korea, South Africa, UEFA Playoff D)
- **Base Camp:** Home soil advantage

## The Story
Mexico are co-hosting their third World Cup — and history says that matters. In 1970 and 1986, they reached the quarter-finals as hosts. The pressure to perform on home soil is immense, but the 12th man advantage is real. El Tri have never been eliminated in the group stage when hosting.

The flip side? Mexico's "quinto partido" curse — the inability to get past the Round of 16 — has defined their modern World Cup history. They've been knocked out in the last 16 in seven consecutive tournaments (1994-2022). This time, at home, the expectation is to finally break through.

## Playing Style
- Tactical discipline with CONCACAF grit
- Strong defensive structure, organized pressing
- Dangerous on set pieces and counter-attacks
- Technical midfield control — comfortable on the ball
- Passionate home support creates a cauldron atmosphere

## Key Players to Watch
- A blend of Liga MX and European-based talent
- Look for their creative midfield playmaker and experienced defensive spine
- Young attackers emerging from European academies add pace and flair

## Strengths
✅ Home advantage — hostile atmosphere for opponents
✅ Never eliminated in groups as hosts
✅ Deep CONCACAF qualifying experience
✅ Passionate fan base that fills every stadium
✅ Tactical organization under consistent coaching philosophy

## Weaknesses
❌ Round of 16 curse — seven consecutive last-16 exits
❌ Pressure of home expectations could weigh heavy
❌ Quality gap vs Europe/South America's elite in knockout rounds
❌ Liga MX-heavy squad may lack top-level European experience

## World Cup History Highlights
- 🏆 1970: Quarter-finals (hosts)
- 🏆 1986: Quarter-finals (hosts)
- 📊 2018: Beat Germany 1-0 in group stage — stunned the world
- 😤 2022: Eliminated in group stage for the first time since 1978

## 💰 Betting Outlook
- **To win the group:** Short odds, near-certain — Group A is favorable
- **To reach Quarter-finals:** Value bet — home advantage + favorable draw could break the curse
- **To win the World Cup:** Longshot (~80/1+) — never say never at home, but quality gap vs elite is real
- **Player props:** Watch for their striker in Golden Boot markets — home games mean goals
