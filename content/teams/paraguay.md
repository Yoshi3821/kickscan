# Paraguay 🇵🇾 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** ~50
- **Confederation:** CONMEBOL
- **World Cup Appearances:** 9
- **Best Result:** Quarter-finals (2010)
- **Group:** D (vs United States, Australia, UEFA Playoff C winner)

## The Story
Paraguay are the ultimate CONMEBOL grinders. They don't dazzle — they suffocate. Defensively disciplined, physically imposing, and masters of the dark arts, Paraguay make every game a battle of attrition. Their 2010 quarter-final run (beating Japan on penalties) showcased this perfectly.

In Group D alongside co-hosts USA, Paraguay are the most dangerous opponent. They know how to frustrate bigger teams and have the CONMEBOL toughness that European and Asian sides struggle against.

## Playing Style
- Defensive discipline — one of South America's hardest teams to break down
- Physical and aggressive — win every 50/50
- Counter-attacking through set pieces and direct play
- Experience in hostile environments — CONMEBOL qualifying is brutal
- Time-wasting, tactical fouls, game management — every tool in the box

## Key Players to Watch
- A blend of South American and European-based talent
- Strong defensive spine with experienced center-backs
- Set-piece specialists who create danger from dead balls

## Strengths
✅ CONMEBOL toughness — they've played Argentina, Brazil, Uruguay in qualifying
✅ 2010 quarter-final pedigree — know how to grind deep runs
✅ Defensively elite — concede very few goals
✅ Nobody wants to play Paraguay — the ultimate "awkward" opponent

## Weaknesses
❌ Limited attacking quality — struggle to score against organized defenses
❌ USA with home advantage is a big challenge
❌ Aging squad in some positions
❌ Not enough European top-league representation
