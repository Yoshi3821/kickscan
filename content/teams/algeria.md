# Algeria 🇩🇿 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** 35
- **Confederation:** CAF
- **World Cup Appearances:** 5 (first since 2014)
- **Best Result:** Round of 16 (2014)
- **Group:** J (vs Argentina, Austria, Jordan)
- **Key Question:** Can the Desert Foxes recapture their 2014 World Cup magic?

## The Story
Algeria's 2014 World Cup was a revelation — they ran Germany ragged in the Round of 16, only losing in extra time. They also won AFCON 2019 under Djamel Belmadi, proving they can peak on the biggest stages. After missing the 2018 and 2022 World Cups, the Desert Foxes return with a point to prove.

Group J is tricky — Argentina are overwhelming favorites and Austria are strong under Rangnick. Algeria will battle Austria for 2nd place in what could be one of the group stage's most tactical encounters.

## Playing Style
- AFCON-winning tactical discipline — organized and hard to break down
- Counter-attacking speed through pacey wingers
- Physical North African intensity — never back down from a challenge
- Set-piece threat — dangerous from dead balls
- Passionate team spirit — the national team means everything

## Key Players to Watch
- **Riyad Mahrez** — The star name, though his form and club status will be key
- **Ismaël Bennacer** — AC Milan midfielder, technical and combative
- **Amine Gouiri** — Rennes attacker, goals and creativity
- **Youcef Atal** — Electric right-back when fit
- A new generation of Ligue 1 and European-based talent

## Strengths
✅ 2019 AFCON champions — winning pedigree
✅ 2014 World Cup showed they can compete with the best (pushed Germany to ET)
✅ Deep pool of French-born dual-national talent
✅ Defensive organization and counter-attacking quality
✅ Massive passionate diaspora — incredible support in North America

## Weaknesses
❌ 12-year World Cup absence — tournament rust is real
❌ Generational transition — 2014/2019 heroes have aged
❌ Argentina and Austria in the group is a tough draw
❌ Inconsistent form between AFCON cycles
❌ Reliance on individual moments rather than sustained quality
