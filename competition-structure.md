# KickScan Competition Structure

## Confirmed by Boss Jay — March 19, 2026

### TWO SEPARATE COMPETITIONS

## 1. 🏟️ LEAGUE SEASON COMPETITION (ongoing, year-round)
- Covers: EPL, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Champions League
- Duration: Follows European football season (Aug → May)
- Resets: Every new season
- Leaderboard: "Season 2025/26 Leaderboard"
- Users predict league matches week by week
- Points accumulate over the full season

## 2. 🏆 WORLD CUP 2026 COMPETITION (special event)
- Covers: ALL 72 group stage matches ONLY (for now)
- Duration: June 11 → July 19, 2026 (tournament dates)
- Separate leaderboard from league competition
- Available in PUBLIC (global) and PRIVATE GROUP formats
- Every user's WC predictions tracked separately
- Can expand to knockout rounds later (32 more matches)

### How It Works Per User:
- Same account, TWO leaderboards
- "My League Rank: #47" + "My World Cup Rank: #12"
- Same points system for both
- Same boosters (2/day shared across both)

### Private Groups (World Cup only):
- Create a group → invite friends via link
- Everyone predicts the 72 WC matches
- Private leaderboard within the group
- Same points system
