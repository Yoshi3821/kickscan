# France 🇫🇷 — Team Profile

## Quick Facts
- **FIFA Ranking:** 3
- **Confederation:** UEFA
- **World Cup Appearances:** 17
- **Best Result:** Winners (1998, 2018), Finalists (2006, 2022)
- **Group:** I (vs Senegal, IC Playoff 2 winner, Norway)
- **Key Question:** Can Mbappé deliver the one trophy he's missing?

## The Story
France are the tournament's co-favorites for a reason. Two World Cup titles, two finals in the last three tournaments, and arguably the deepest squad in football history. Mbappé — now the undisputed face of football post-Messi/Ronaldo — leads a generation that includes Tchouaméni, Camavinga, Dembélé, and Saliba. The talent is obscene.

But France also carry baggage. The 2022 final loss to Argentina on penalties was agonizing — Mbappé scored a hat-trick and still lost. There's unfinished business. France have reached the semi-finals or better in 4 of the last 5 major tournaments. They don't just compete — they contend.

## Playing Style
- Devastating pace on the counter — Mbappé, Dembélé create nightmare scenarios
- Physically dominant — size, speed, power across every position
- Tactical flexibility — can play possession or counter-attacking football
- Individual brilliance can unlock any defense
- Experienced tournament management under French coaching tradition

## Key Players to Watch
- **Kylian Mbappé** — The world's best player. Speed, finishing, big-game mentality. This is his tournament.
- **Aurélien Tchouaméni** — Real Madrid's midfield anchor, world-class
- **Eduardo Camavinga** — Versatile, dynamic, Champions League winner
- **William Saliba** — Arsenal's rock, elite defensive reading
- **Ousmane Dembélé** — Inconsistent genius — when he's on, he's unplayable

## Strengths
✅ Deepest squad in the tournament — can field two competitive XIs
✅ Mbappé is the best player at the World Cup
✅ 4 semi-finals in last 5 major tournaments — ultimate tournament team
✅ Physical dominance — nobody outmuscles France
✅ Big-game experience from multiple finals

## Weaknesses
❌ Internal politics — French squad drama is a recurring risk (2010 mutiny, occasional ego clashes)
❌ Can be complacent in "easy" group games — slow starts
❌ Over-reliance on individual moments rather than system play
❌ Defensive vulnerabilities when pressed high (Argentina exploited this in 2022)
❌ The weight of expectation — France are always favorites, which brings pressure

## World Cup History Highlights
- 🏆 1998: Winners (hosts — Zidane masterclass)
- 🏆 2018: Winners (Mbappé's breakthrough at 19)
- 🥈 2022: Finalists (Mbappé hat-trick in final, lost on penalties to Argentina)
- 🥈 2006: Finalists (Zidane headbutt final)
- 😤 2002: Defending champions, eliminated in groups with 0 goals scored
